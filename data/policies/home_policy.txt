HOME SHIELD INSURANCE POLICY

Section 1: Water Damage
Accidental water damage caused by burst pipes is covered.

Damage resulting from negligence, lack of maintenance, or gradual leakage is not covered.

Section 2: Fire Damage
Fire damage caused by electrical faults, lightning, or accidental causes is covered.

Intentional fire caused by the policyholder is excluded.

Section 3: Theft
Theft is covered only if there is evidence of forced entry.

Maximum payout for theft is $50,000.

Section 4: Flood
Flood damage is excluded unless Flood Endorsement FE-101 is attached.
