CLAIM ADJUDICATION RULES

A claim can be approved only if:

1. The loss is covered.
2. The claimant has an active policy.
3. Required endorsements are present.
4. Fraud indicators are absent.

A claim must be escalated to a human adjuster if:

- Multiple conflicting policy clauses exist.
- Retrieved context is insufficient.
- Confidence is low.
- State regulations are required.
